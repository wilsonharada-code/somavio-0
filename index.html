What matters first
🐻
Stay calm. Do not run.
Most visitor safety decisions begin with slowing down, keeping distance and avoiding sudden movement. Running, crowding wildlife or stopping for photos can make a calm moment worse.
👥
Create space and choose a safer plan
Follow posted closures, give wildlife room, keep your group together and switch to a safer route or indoor option when alerts are active.
